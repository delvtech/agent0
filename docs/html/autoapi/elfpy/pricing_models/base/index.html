Calculates the spot price of base in terms of bonds.

\[\begin{align} p = (\frac{2y + cz}{\mu z})^{-\tau} \end{align}\]

  • time_remaining (StretchedTime) – The time remaining for the asset (incorporates time stretch).

Returns:
